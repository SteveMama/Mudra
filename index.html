    MVP notes

    What works now

    Continuous detection: automatic sign boundary detection via wrist velocity, ST-GCN word inference per segment, gesture recognition layer, and phrase accumulation with multilingual output.

    Letter layer

    The built-in gesture model covers 8 shapes. For full ASL fingerspelling, integrate the Kaggle ASL Fingerspelling 1st-place Squeezeformer (Apache-2.0, TFLite, MediaPipe-native).

    Phrase layer

    Integrate VIPL-SLP/MSLR_ICCV2025 (ICCV 2025, Google Drive checkpoints) for CTC-based continuous phrase recognition, then fine-tune on How2Sign or WLASL pose extracts for ASL.
